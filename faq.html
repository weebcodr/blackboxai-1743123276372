Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to start flight training?

You must be at least 16 years old to fly solo and 17 to get your private pilot license. You'll need a medical certificate from an FAA-approved doctor.

How long does it take to get a private pilot license?

Most students complete their training in 6-12 months, depending on frequency of lessons. The FAA requires a minimum of 40 flight hours.

What is the cost of flight training?

Private pilot training typically costs between $10,000-$15,000. We offer flexible payment plans and financing options.

Can I fly if I wear glasses or contacts?

Yes! Many pilots wear corrective lenses. Your medical exam will determine if your vision meets FAA standards.

Do you offer discovery flights?

Yes! A discovery flight is a great way to experience flying before committing to lessons. Book with any of our instructors.
